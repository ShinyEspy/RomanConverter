Rules & Notes

  • Supports standard Roman numerals in the range 1–3999.
  • Uses subtractive notation (e.g., IV=4, IX=9, XL=40, CM=900).
  • Validates Roman numerals using a canonical pattern; non-canonical forms (e.g., IIII) are rejected.
